FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How accurate is the AI risk assessment?

Our AI system achieves high accuracy rates across different medical conditions, typically above 95% for most assessments. The system combines multiple machine learning models with clinical knowledge to provide reliable risk assessments.

What data is required for risk assessment?

The system requires basic patient information such as age, gender, BMI, blood pressure, and condition-specific biomarkers. The more data available, the more accurate the assessment becomes.

Is patient data secure?

Absolutely. We follow strict data security protocols and comply with healthcare data protection regulations. All patient data is encrypted and processed securely.

Can the system handle multiple patients at once?

Yes, our system supports bulk assessment of multiple patients simultaneously. This feature is particularly useful for healthcare facilities that need to process large numbers of patients efficiently.

How do I get started with the system?

Simply visit our risk assessment page and enter patient data using any of our input methods: manual entry, JSON format, or bulk upload. The system will provide immediate risk assessment results.

Do you offer training and support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training and ongoing support to help healthcare professionals make the most of our AI system. Contact us to learn more about our training programs.
